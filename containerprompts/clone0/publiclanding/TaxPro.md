# TAX PRO PROGRAM — FREELANCE TAX PREPARERS

---

## OVERVIEW

Tax4U allows independent tax preparers to join the platform as freelance Tax Pros.

This is designed for professionals who:

- already prepare taxes  
- want more clients  
- want a structured system  
- want to work independently with support  

---

## WHO THIS IS FOR

This program is ideal if you:

- are an experienced tax preparer  
- handle W-2, 1099, or mixed income cases  
- want to grow your client base  
- want to operate as a freelancer  
- want access to tools and workflow support  

---

## WHAT YOU GET

As a Tax Pro inside Tax4U, you get:

- access to clients through the platform  
- structured workflow (Step1 → Step10 system)  
- organized document management (R2 + system)  
- AI-assisted case preparation (Step3AI)  
- secure communication with clients  
- scalable workflow without needing your own infrastructure  

---

## HOW IT WORKS

1. You register as a Tax Pro  
2. You receive or manage assigned cases  
3. You review documents and client data  
4. You prepare tax returns  
5. You complete the process with the client  

---

## IMPORTANT PRINCIPLES

- You remain an independent freelancer  
- You manage your own workload  
- The platform supports your process  
- The system helps organize, not replace your expertise  

---
